Made in: Game Maker Designed for: Mobile Genre: Arcade

You Can't Jump is a very small anti-platformer that I developed independently over 2 weeks. In it you play as an evil minion who can only walk left and right, and must avoid oncoming attacks from the heros.


The gameplay is very simple, but I came up with ways to keep it interesting for the player. The game has a high score system, gaining 1 point for each hero evaded and reaching certain scores will unlock either new levels to play on or different music. You can also die several ways from being jumped on, eaten, falling off. Each of these are recorded, and if a player is not capable of getting a high enough score to unlock the new levels, getting killed often will unlock the levels too.


There are also areas that I would like to expand on in this game. The different levels are just skins and don't affect enemy spawns or controls, but this could be changed to make it more interesting. Plus I never properly optimised the game to run on different mobile devices, which would improve the game’s quality. Yet despite these shortcomings I do still feel a created a small and fun experience.
