Mad Scientist Attack Of The Gnomes
A 2D puzzle platformer where the player must control a variety of different animals in order to recapture those that have escaped from the Scientist. The player does have to be careful though no to kill the animals they wish to recapture. A first-person tower defence style game where you need to defend yourself from attacking Gnomes by laying traps. As opposed to other Tower Defence games, the player is the tower and can move around to avoid being conquered.
