StarratS is the first game that I made for mobile devices. It is a vertical scrolling shooter about a Titanium Orbiting Mouse calle Jeri who has been enslaved by the StarratS for centuries. One day she has had enough and decides to rise up against her suppressors.


The majority of the artwork was done by my friend Heather Poole, but all coding and game ideas were my own. It features an ingame currency system of cheese which can be used to upgrade Jeri, giving her better guns, more health, improved maneuverability and so forth. You also start with a limited number of missiles which can clear all enemies on the screen in one shot and move can be purchased with cheese.


The enemies come in a variety of different types and in different randomly generated attack waves. As the game progresses and you pass the bosses, then the challenge increases by having attack waves appear more quickly and in stronger formations.


There are various collectable elements to pick up during gameplay. Enemies will occasionally drop powerups to give you a temporary boost such as shields, multi guns or speed boost. They will also drop cheese to buy the permanent upgrades. But occasionally a shooting star will appear in screen which is a collectable item for the in-game observatory. Each star collected will be added to a constellation, and once all the stars for the constellation are complete the player will be rewarded with imagery and some information.
